What is Mindfulness?

Mindfulness is the practice of purposely bringing one's attention to the present moment without judgment. It helps us become more aware of our thoughts, feelings, bodily sensations, and surrounding environment.

Regular mindfulness practice can reduce stress, improve focus, and help manage difficult emotions. The exercises below can be practiced anywhere and only take a few minutes.

Quick Tips

  • Start with short sessions (3-5 minutes)
  • Practice regularly, even if briefly
  • Your mind will wander - that's normal
  • Gently return focus when distracted
  • Be patient and non-judgmental with yourself

When to Practice

Try incorporating mindfulness into your daily routine:

  • In the morning to set a positive tone for the day
  • During a work break to reset your focus
  • When feeling stressed or overwhelmed
  • Before bed to help quiet your mind
  • While doing everyday activities like eating or walking
